Poetry is a huge part of who I am. I would say that one of my strengths is writing Khmer poetry. When I came to Liger, I was introduced to English poetry. One thing that I notice about the difference between Khmer and English poetry is that rhyming doesn’t necessary in English poetry.

Here is a palindrome poem that I wrote last year.

“My life can end easily”

It’s just a lie saying that,

I’m Not gonna give up or commit suicide

I strongly push my mind to think about

Being stuck in this dark world

There’s no way that I can’t escape

Depression. sticks in my brain

And

Opportunities stop coming

I 100% don’t believe that

People can't cut my wings and they can’t control my freedom

That always works in my mind

LOSE my HOPE!

The thing is that I won’t

IGNORE

Your Sharp Bullet

You know? Your words

(to) Stop myself from my passion

I believe I won’t be able

To Have a Positive Journey

I always think about

Giving up

I can’t move on

I strongly disagree that

The world is just!


But,

I changed my mind

Read it backward!
